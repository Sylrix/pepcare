Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Shipping Policy explains how PepCare processes, packages, and ships research materials, and outlines the responsibilities of the receiver. Because many of our products are temperature-sensitive, proper handling and prompt receipt are essential to maintaining product integrity.

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation and successful eligibility/compliance verification. Processing times may be longer for custom items, large orders, periods of high demand, or where additional verification is required.

Orders are dispatched on business days only. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day. You will receive a confirmation once your order ships.

2. Cold-Chain and Packaging

Temperature-sensitive products are packaged to maintain stability during transit using appropriate methods such as insulated containers, gel ice packs, or dry ice, depending on the product's requirements.

  • Cold-chain shipments are timed to minimize transit duration and are typically shipped using expedited services;
  • Upon arrival, cold-chain products must be inspected and transferred to appropriate storage immediately;
  • Coolants such as dry ice are intended only to maintain temperature in transit and may have partially or fully dissipated on arrival without affecting product integrity, provided the item is promptly stored correctly.

All products are packaged in compliance with applicable transport and safety regulations.

3. Carriers

We ship via established, reputable carriers selected based on destination, product requirements, and transit speed. For temperature-sensitive or time-critical shipments, we use expedited or specialized services as needed. The carrier and service level for your order are determined at the time of dispatch and may be reflected in your shipping confirmation.

4. Shipping Options & Rates

We offer both domestic (United States) and international shipping where permitted by law. The following flat shipping rates are presented and selected at checkout:

  • Standard — $14.00: typically 3–5 business days after dispatch, fully tracked.
  • Express — $20.00: typically 1–2 business days after dispatch, with priority cold-chain handling for temperature-sensitive items.

Transit estimates begin after dispatch and are not guaranteed once a package is with the carrier. International shipments may take longer due to distance, carrier routing, and customs clearance, and certain products may be available for domestic shipping only or subject to additional restrictions for international destinations. Any customs duties, import taxes, or brokerage fees are the buyer's responsibility.

5. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international orders, the buyer is the importer of record and is solely responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, VAT, brokerage fees, or other charges imposed by the destination country. These charges are not included in the product price or shipping fee and are collected by the carrier or customs authority.

Buyers are responsible for ensuring that the products they order may be lawfully imported into their country and that they hold any required permits or authorizations. We are not responsible for delays, seizures, or refusals by customs authorities, and we do not refund charges arising from customs processing.

6. Restricted Destinations

We do not ship to destinations where the sale, import, or possession of a given product is prohibited or restricted by applicable law, export controls, or sanctions. We reserve the right to refuse or cancel any order to a destination we determine to be restricted, high-risk, or non-compliant.

It is the buyer's responsibility to confirm that the products ordered are legal to receive and use in their jurisdiction. Orders to restricted destinations will be cancelled and refunded in accordance with our policies.

7. Tracking

Once your order ships, we will provide a tracking number where available so you can monitor its progress. For cold-chain shipments, we strongly recommend tracking your package closely and arranging for someone to be available to receive and store it immediately upon delivery.

8. Lost or Delayed Packages

While we make every effort to ensure timely delivery, transit times are estimates and are not guaranteed once a package is in the carrier's custody. Delays may occur due to weather, customs, carrier issues, or other factors beyond our control.

  • If your package appears lost or significantly delayed, contact us promptly so we can assist in tracing it with the carrier;
  • Claims for lost shipments are subject to carrier investigation timelines;
  • For temperature-sensitive items, delivery delays caused by the buyer's unavailability or by customs are at the buyer's risk.

9. Receiver Responsibility

By placing an order, the receiver agrees to:

  • Provide an accurate, complete shipping address and ensure someone is available to receive the shipment;
  • Inspect shipments promptly on arrival and report any damage in line with our Refund & Returns Policy;
  • Immediately store products under their required conditions (e.g., refrigerated or frozen) using appropriate, calibrated laboratory equipment;
  • Handle all products as hazardous research materials, following the relevant Safety Data Sheet (SDS/MSDS) and using suitable personal protective equipment and facilities.

PepCare is not responsible for product degradation or loss resulting from improper storage, handling, or delayed receipt after delivery.
