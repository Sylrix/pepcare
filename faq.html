Frequently asked questions

Everything about Research Use Only status, documentation, shipping, and ordering. Still stuck? Contact our team.

What does Research Use Only (RUO) mean?
All PepCare products are intended exclusively for in-vitro laboratory research. They are not drugs, supplements, or medical devices, and are not for human or veterinary consumption, clinical, diagnostic, or therapeutic use.
Do your products come with a Certificate of Analysis?
Yes. Every batch ships with a lot-specific Certificate of Analysis. You can also review COAs on each product page before ordering, including identity and purity results.
Who performs your testing?
Identity and purity are verified by independent, accredited third-party laboratories using HPLC and mass spectrometry. We publish the data rather than relying on self-reported figures.
How are products shipped and stored?
Temperature-sensitive compounds ship in validated cold-chain packaging. Each product page lists recommended storage conditions so you can preserve stability once it reaches your lab.
Who is allowed to order?
Orders are accepted only from qualified researchers, laboratories, and institutions for legitimate research purposes. By ordering, you confirm you are purchasing for research use and will not use products for human or animal consumption.
What is your returns policy?
Because these are sensitive research materials, returns are limited. If an order arrives damaged, incorrect, or out of specification, contact support promptly and we will arrange a replacement or resolution. See our Refund & Returns Policy.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship to many regions, subject to local regulations and import requirements. Some compounds may be restricted in certain jurisdictions — the buyer is responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable local laws. See our Shipping Policy.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major payment methods through a secure, encrypted, PCI-compliant checkout. Institutional purchase orders may be available for qualified accounts — contact us to set one up.
Do you offer bulk pricing or custom quotes?
Yes. For larger quantities, recurring orders, or custom requirements, request a quote and our team will prepare pricing and lead times tailored to your project.
