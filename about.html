Our story

Built by scientists, for scientists

PepCare was founded by scientists who were tired of inconsistent materials and missing paperwork derailing good experiments. We built the supplier we always wanted: rigorous third-party testing, transparent batch-level documentation, and logistics that respect the chemistry.

Today we serve academic labs, core facilities, and biotech teams who measure us by the reproducibility of their results.

Our quality commitment

Every batch is tested by accredited third-party laboratories using HPLC and mass spectrometry, released only when it meets specification, and shipped with a lot-specific Certificate of Analysis. If material does not meet our standard, it does not ship.

RUO Compliance & intended use

PepCare products are sold strictly for Research Use Only. They are not for human or veterinary consumption and make no medical, therapeutic, or diagnostic claims. Products are sold only to qualified researchers and institutions for legitimate laboratory research, and buyers are responsible for handling, storage, and use in accordance with applicable laws and institutional policies.
