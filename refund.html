Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At PepCare, we want you to receive your research materials accurately, intact, and in suitable condition. Because our products are sensitive research chemicals and biochemical reference materials, returns are necessarily limited for safety, quality, and integrity reasons. This policy explains when refunds or replacements are available and how to start a claim.

Return policy summary

We accept returns and exchanges (replacements) for defective, damaged, or incorrect items only. Report the issue within 7 days of delivery; a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) is required before sending anything back. The customer arranges and pays return shipping, except where the fault is our error or the item was damaged in transit. Opened, used, or temperature-sensitive (cold-chain) items are non-returnable. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping to: United States.

1. Order Accuracy

We take care to ensure every order is picked, prepared, and shipped correctly. Upon receipt, please inspect your order promptly and verify that the products, quantities, and specifications match your order confirmation.

If you believe there is an error in your order, contact us as soon as possible so we can investigate and resolve the issue. Please retain all packaging, labels, and documentation until the matter is resolved.

2. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, you must notify us within 7 calendar days of delivery. To process your claim, please provide:

  • Your order number;
  • A clear description of the issue;
  • Photographs of the affected product, labels, and outer/inner packaging, including any visible damage;
  • Where relevant, evidence of the condition of cold-chain packaging on arrival.

Verified claims for damaged or incorrect items will be resolved by replacement or refund at our discretion. Claims submitted after the 7-day window may not be eligible.

3. Conditions for Returns of Research Materials

Due to the sensitive nature of research chemicals and biochemicals, returns are accepted only under strict conditions. A return may be considered only where the item is:

  • Unopened and unused, with all original tamper-evident seals and labeling fully intact;
  • Authorized in advance by us via a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA);
  • Returned within the window stated in your RMA.

Cold-chain caveat: Products that require temperature-controlled storage (refrigerated, frozen, or shipped on dry ice / ice packs) generally cannot be returned once delivered, because their stability and integrity cannot be guaranteed once they leave our controlled custody. Such items are eligible for refund or replacement only in cases of verified shipping damage or our error.

4. Non-Returnable Items

For safety, quality, and regulatory reasons, the following are not returnable and are not eligible for refund except in the case of our error or verified shipping damage:

  • Opened, used, or partially used products;
  • Products with broken or missing tamper-evident seals;
  • Temperature-sensitive / cold-chain products once delivered;
  • Custom, made-to-order, or specially synthesized items;
  • Products returned without a valid RMA;
  • Products that have been stored or handled outside of their required conditions after delivery.

5. Refund Method and Timing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note:

  • Refunds are processed after we receive and inspect any returned item, or after a claim is verified for damaged/incorrect goods;
  • We typically initiate approved refunds within 5 to 10 business days of approval;
  • The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your bank or card issuer;
  • Original shipping charges are generally non-refundable except where the return results from our error or a defective product.

6. Order Cancellations

You may request to cancel an order before it has been dispatched. Once an order has been processed or shipped, it can no longer be cancelled and will be subject to this Refund & Returns Policy. Custom or specially prepared orders may not be cancellable once production has begun. We also reserve the right to cancel any order in accordance with our Terms of Service and Compliance Policy, in which case any payment taken will be refunded.

7. How to Start a Claim

To request a return, replacement, or refund, please contact us with your order details:

  • Email: legal@pepcare.com
  • Include your order number, a description of the issue, and supporting photographs where applicable.

Do not ship any item back without first receiving a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA). Unauthorized returns may be refused and may not be eligible for refund. All returns must comply with applicable shipping, safety, and transport regulations for the materials involved.
